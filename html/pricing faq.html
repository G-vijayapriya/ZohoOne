Pricing FAQ

How many different Zoho One plans are available?

We now have 2 plans:

Flexible-User Pricing

We offer Flexible-User Pricing at ₹4,000 per user per month, or ₹3,500 per user per month with an annual subscription. This means you can buy any number of user licenses; you do not have to purchase licenses for ALL employees.

All Employee Pricing

We offer the All Employee Pricing at a special price of ₹2,500 per employee per month, or ₹1,250 per employee per month with an annual subscription, provided you purchase licenses for ALL employees in the company.

Do I need to buy a Zoho One license for every employee in my organization?

No, you can purchase as many Zoho One licenses as you need under the Flexible User Pricing plan. The requirement to buy a license for every employee in your organization applies only to the All-Employee Pricing plan.

Is there a minimum license requirement? Can I get the All-Employee plan if I'm a sole proprietor/one-person business?

There is no minimum amount of licenses for either plan. You can get the cheaper All-Employee plan even if you are just a one-person business.

Which pricing model do you suggest I choose?

No. The Flexible-User Pricing plan is available for these situations. With this plan you can purchase the exact number of Zoho One licenses you need. If you have any questions, please contact our sales team.

I have employees who don't use a computer or mobile phone. Do I need a Zoho One license for those employees?

No. The Flexible-User Pricing plan is available for these situations. With this plan you can purchase the exact number of Zoho One licenses you need. If you have any questions, please contact our sales team.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept online payments through Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. If you don't have a credit card, we accept checks, PayPal (you must have a PayPal account), and wire transfers. To arrange offline payments, or if you have any questions, please contact our sales team.

Are there long-term contracts?

No, we don't force you into any long-term contracts. Our best price is available as an annual subscription, but we offer monthly subscriptions as well.

Can I add or remove Zoho One licenses before my contract is up?

Yes, you can add or remove licenses online or by contacting our sales team. After removing a license you can contact our payments team for an appropriate refund.

I currently have a contract with Zoho and switching to Zoho One will save me money. How will you credit me or prorate the difference?

If moving to Zoho One saves you money, you'll automatically receive a prorated price when upgrading to it. Please note: To automatically receive a prorated price, you'll need to first start a trial of Zoho One, add the applications you're currently using, and then upgrade.

I have multiple Zoho apps on different billing cycles. Will this affect my ability to upgrade to Zoho One?

No, it won't. When you upgrade to Zoho One, our system will automatically consolidate your billing, give you the adjusted rate, and provide a single payment date. Please note: To automatically receive adjusted billing, you'll need to first start a trial of Zoho One, add the applications you're currently using, and then upgrade.
